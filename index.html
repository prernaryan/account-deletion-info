How to Delete Your Account in MedCuratio

We value your privacy and want to ensure that you have control over your personal data. Follow the steps below to request the deletion of your account and associated data in MedCuratio.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the MedCuratio app on your device.
  2. Go to the profile section on the profile icon.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom.
  4. Tap on "Delete Account".
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion.

Data Deletion Information:

Deleted Data: All personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted, including your profile information, activity history, and any stored preferences.
